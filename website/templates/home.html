Music of India

Indian music is a diverse and rich art form that has evolved over thousands of years. It is characterized by its intricate melodies, complex rhythms, and a wide variety of instruments, both traditional and modern. One of the most notable features of Indian music is its use of ragas, which are melodic frameworks that define the mood, emotion, and character of a piece of music. Each raga is associated with a specific time of day or season, and is meant to evoke a particular emotion or feeling in the listener. Over the years, Indian music has influenced many other musical traditions around the world, and continues to be a vibrant and dynamic art form that is constantly evolving..

Carnatic

Carnatic music is a classical music tradition from South India that is known for its intricate melodies, rhythmic complexities, improvisations, and emphasis on vocal performances accompanied by various traditional instruments.
